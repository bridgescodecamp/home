What is a coder's day like

Being in Front of a Computer

A typical day is at least 8 hours or more in front of a computer.

And lots of typing, key combinations, and use a the mouse.

Reading and More Reading

Looking up definitions, tutorials and documentation is non stop.

Using google and reading question and answers can happen several times in an hour.

Organizing and Naming

Categorizing, naming and organizing are critical to a coders success.

Do you love to organize your closet, are you good at filing?

Learning and Problem Solving

Every day a coder learns something new and there is always more to learn at any skill level.

Finding simple understandable solutions to complex problems is at the heart of being a coder.

What makes a good coder

Patience and Courage

You will really need patience to deal with the adversity that comes with learning to code.

At times, things can feel very foreign, awkward, and frustrating. By sticking with it you will be surprised with what you can accomplish.

Creativity

Thinking out of the box or arriving at your own solution are very important.

There is a lot of common ways of coding but they all can be applied in your own creative way.

Passion

Passion is what will keep you up all night until you solve a problem.

Having a burning desire to learn is a must.

Problem Solving

Do you like suduko, chess or nintendo games? Do you fill fulfilled after solving a tough problem?

Most coders have an inherent desire to build, disassemble, question and conquer challenges.
